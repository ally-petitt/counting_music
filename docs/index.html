About Us

You must be here because you love music. I am here because I love to teach music. Band over a distance is just not the same as in-person. After experiencing first-hand the challenges of black screens and muted microphones, I can state with certainty that we need a way to better reach our students. This is why I created countingmusic.com. Teacher by day, web developer by night.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide any and everyone with access to quality music education. I'm sure you understand the beauty of melodies and harmonies, and I'd imagine that you are pretty interested in music if you are reading this right now. That is our goal here at CountingMusic.com. Everyone deserves quality music, we are here to help you get it.
