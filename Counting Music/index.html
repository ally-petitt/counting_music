About Us

You must be here because you love music. I am here because I love to teach music. I'm sure we've all heard at least one song that gave us chills, or maybe one that brought us tears. This is the power of music, and it is why I love it so much. I am a high school band teacher by day, web developer by night.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide any and everyone with access to quality music education. I'm sure you understand the beauty of melodies and harmonies, especially since you are reading this right now, so you must also understand the importance of spreading this gift. That is our goal here at CountingMusic.com. Everyone deserves quality music, we are here to help you get it.
